APP REVIEW NOTES (Englisch, falls gefragt)
─────────────────────────────────────────

Room8 is a student housing platform for finding shared apartments (WGs), student jobs, local events and student discount coupons in Germany.

To test the full experience, please use the demo account above. The app includes:
- Roommate matching with preference-based score
- Coupons redeemable at local partner stores
- Student job listings
- Local event listings
- In-app chat between users
- Push notifications for new matches and messages

All user-generated content can be reported and removed. The app is GDPR-compliant. Account deletion is available in-app under Settings.
